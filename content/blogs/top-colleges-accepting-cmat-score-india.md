## What is CMAT and Why It Is One of the Most Important MBA Admission Tests  
  
CMAT (Common Management Admission Test) is a standardized test for MBA conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). The score obtained in the exam is valid for AICTE, approved MBA and PGDM colleges in India.

#### Reasons for CMAT being a significant factor for MBA:

| Reason | Benefit for Students |
| --- | --- |
| Accepted by 1000+ Colleges | More MBA admission opportunities across India |
| Lower Competition than CAT | Better chances of shortlisting |
| Affordable Exam Fee | Budget-friendly entrance exam |
| Accepted by Top Institutes | Valid for government & private colleges |
| Open to All Candidates | Suitable for freshers and professionals |
| Valid for MBA & PGDM | Wider course options |

## FAQs CMAT MBA Admissions  

**Question 1: Is CMAT easier than CAT?**
>Yes, it is a common perception that CMAT is less difficult than CAT because the quant and verbal sections are not as complicated.

**Question 2: Can I get top MBA colleges with CMAT?**

>Yes, colleges like JBIMS, SIMSREE, Welingkar, and PUMBA, among others, accept CMAT scores (based on the state regulations).

**Question 3: Is CMAT accepted for PGDM programs?**
> It is true that a number of AICTE, approved PGDM colleges accept CMAT scores.

**Question 4: Can I apply to government colleges through CMAT?**

>There are quite a few government and university departments that accept CMAT for MBA admission.

**Question 5: What is a good CMAT score? ?**

> A score higher than 95 percentile is already great, however, colleges of a good level may be available even with an 80+ percentile score.
