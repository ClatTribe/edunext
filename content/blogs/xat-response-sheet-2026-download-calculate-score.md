### Manual Score Calculation Formula

**(Expected Score) = (Correct Answers × 1) – (Wrong Answers × 0.25)**

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Correct Answers | 55 |

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs): XAT Response Sheet 2026

### 1. What is the XAT Response Sheet 2026?

The XAT Response Sheet 2026 is an official document released by XLRI that shows the answers marked by a candidate during the XAT exam. It helps students compare their responses with the official answer key and calculate their expected score before the final results are declared.

### 2. When will XAT Response Sheet 2026 be released?

The XAT response sheet is usually released within 2–3 days after the exam. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official XAT website to download it as the availability window is limited.

### 3. How can I download my XAT Response Sheet 2026?

You can download your response sheet by:

- Visiting the official XAT website
- Logging in using your XAT ID and Date of Birth
- Clicking on the XAT Response Sheet 2026 link
- Downloading the PDF for future reference

### 4. How do I calculate my XAT score using the response sheet?

To calculate your score:

- Add +1 mark for each correct answer
- Deduct 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer
- Deduct 0.10 marks for more than 8 unattempted questions

**Note:** The General Knowledge section is not counted for percentile calculation.

### 5. What is the XAT Score Calculator 2026?

The XAT Score Calculator 2026 is a tool that helps candidates quickly calculate their expected raw score using their response sheet and the official marking scheme. It reduces calculation errors and saves time.
