## FAQs

**Q1. Is 92 a good score in CLAT?**  
> A score of 92 in the CLAT 2026 examination is definitely a good score and most probably be able to secure a rank between 200 and 600.
With this score, candidates are likely to get admission in some good National Law Universities (NLUs) such as WBNUJS Kolkata, NLU Jodhpur, GNLU Gandhinagar, and maybe NALSAR Hyderabad with about 75% chances.

**Q2. Is online coaching effective for CLAT 2027?**  
> Yes. Online CLAT coaching is highly effective when it offers structured study plans, expert mentorship, regular mock analysis, and exam-focused GK coverage. With CLAT becoming more analytical, online platforms provide better flexibility and deeper concept clarity than traditional offline coaching..

**Q3. How important is GK in CLAT 2027, and how does online coaching help?**  
> GK remains a rank-deciding section in CLAT. Online coaching platforms offer:
Monthly GK capsules
Daily newspaper summaries
Weekly quizzes
Flashcards for retention
These tools ensure consistent, stress-free GK preparation.

**Q4. Do online CLAT courses provide doubt-solving and mentorship??**  
> Yes. Quality online CLAT coaching includes live doubt-solving sessions, one-on-one mentorship, performance reviews, and guidance to help students stay motivated and avoid preparation burnout.
