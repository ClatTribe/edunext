## Entrance Exams Accepted by Top MBA Colleges

Most top MBA colleges in India accept one or more of the following exams:

- **CAT** – IIMs, FMS, SPJIMR, MDI, IIFT, JBIMS
- **XAT** – XLRI, XIMB, IMI
- **GMAT/GRE** – ISB, SPJIMR, Great Lakes
- **NMAT** – NMIMS Mumbai
- **SNAP** – SIBM Pune
- **MAH-CET** – JBIMS, SIMSREE

## Fees vs Placements: Understanding MBA ROI in India

A common mistake aspirants make is focusing only on fees or only on packages. The real metric is Return on Investment (ROI).

### High ROI MBA Colleges

- **FMS Delhi** – Extremely low fees, top-tier placements
- **JBIMS Mumbai** – Government-backed fees with premium roles
- **TISS Mumbai** – Strong ROI with diverse career paths
- **IIM Mumbai** – Excellent ROI in operations & consulting

### Premium MBA Colleges

- IIM ABC, ISB, SPJIMR charge higher fees
- Offer leadership roles, global exposure, and strong alumni leverage
