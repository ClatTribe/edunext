# All About CMAT | A Complete Guide to the CMAT Exam, Eligibility, Top Colleges, and Admission Process

### What is CMAT?

**CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)** is an MBA entrance exam at the national level led by the **National Testing Agency (NTA)**. Here is a brief: 

* There are more than **1000+** AICTE approved MBA and PGDM colleges in India that accept CMAT.

* CMAT is only second to CAT in terms of popularity among MBA entrance exams.

It facilitates admission to the following programs:

-MBA

-PGDM

-PGDM Executive

### Reasons to Choose CMAT for MBA Admission?

* Direct access to premier MBA colleges that provide excellent ROI

* Open more doors for admission than only a few CAT colleges

* Recognized by leading B, schools in Maharashtra such as JBIMS and SIMSREE

* Many prestigious private management institutes prefer it

* Ideal for both final, year students and working professionals

### CMAT Eligibility Criteria

| Eligibility Factor | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Educational Qualification | • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university |
| Minimum Marks Required | • General Category: 50% or above• Reserved Categories: 45% or above (as per institute norms) |
| Final Year Students | • Students appearing in the final year of graduation are eligible to apply |
| Age Limit | • No age limit for appearing in CMAT |
| Who Should Apply? | • Suitable for fresh graduates• Ideal for working professionals planning career growth |

### CMAT Exam Pattern & Details

 

  
**CMAT Exam Details**  
  

| Particulars | Details |
| --- | --- |
| CMAT Full Form | Common Management Admission Test |
| Conducted By | National Testing Agency (NTA) |
| Level of Exam | National Level |
| Exam Frequency | Once a year |
| Mode of Exam | Computer-Based Test (Online) |
| Syllabus | Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Language Comprehension, General Awareness, Innovation & Entrepreneurship |
| Number of Sections in Exam | 5 Sections |
| Exam Pattern | Objective Type (MCQs) |
| Language | English |
| Marking Scheme | +4 for each correct answer, –1 for each wrong answer |
| Duration of the Exam | 3 Hours (180 Minutes) |
| Official Website | [Click here](https://cmat.nta.nic.in/) |

  
  
**CMAT Exam Structure**  
  

| Section | No. of Questions |
| --- | --- |
| Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation | 20 |
| Logical Reasoning | 20 |
| Language Comprehension | 20 |
| General Awareness | 20 |
| Innovation & Entrepreneurship | 20 |
| Total | 100 Questions |

  
### CMAT vs CAT vs XAT  
  

| Factor | CMAT | CAT | XAT |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Difficulty Level | Moderate | High | High |
| Colleges Covered | 1000+ | Mostly IIMs & top B-schools | XLRI + Tier-1 |
| Competition | Medium | Very High | High |

### Steps to Get Admission in CMAT Colleges After the Exam

Unlike CAT, CMAT does not have a centralized counseling process. Students need to apply college, wise.

**Stepwise Admission Process**

* Declaration of CMAT Result by NTA
    
* Sending Applications to CMAT Score Accepting Colleges
    
* Shortlisting on the Basis of CMAT Percentile
    
* GD, PI interview 
    
* Final Merit List & Letter of Offer
    
* Confirm Admission by Paying Fees
    

One of the biggest errors CMAT aspirants make is missing deadlines. Submitting applications timely is very important.
