## About First Bridge Business School

Most MBA programs are built around a simple idea: teach business theory, and the market will sort out who gets hired. First Bridge Business School in Gurgaon was built around a different question — what do companies actually need when they hire a fresh management graduate, and how do you design a program that delivers exactly that?

The answer they landed on is an applied, industry-immersive curriculum that works backwards from real job roles rather than forward from academic syllabi. Every course at First Bridge produces a portfolio-ready output — a strategy deck, a marketing campaign, a business model, a data dashboard — that students can show a recruiter on day one of placement season.

The school is led by Dr. Rajeev Roy, an IIM Ahmedabad alumnus and former Dean at XLRI Delhi NCR, with a faculty team that has a median industry experience of 24 years. The faculty is practitioner-heavy: CXOs, founders, consultants, and senior operators — people who have solved the kinds of problems they are teaching.

The campus sits on a 50-acre smart campus in Sector 32, Gurgaon — which means students are not isolated from the corporate world they are preparing to enter. GCCs, unicorn startups, and multinational firms are all within reach. That proximity is a deliberate choice, not a coincidence.

## Programs offered

First Bridge currently runs two full-time post-graduate management programs.

### PGP — Post Graduate Program in Management (2 years)

The flagship program is designed for fresh graduates or candidates with up to 2 years of work experience. It runs over 4 terms across 2 years, with Year 1 covering business fundamentals and applied projects, and Year 2 going deeper into specialisations, a 6-month full-time internship, and optional global opportunities.

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| Duration | 2 years full-time |
| Batch size | 120 students |
| Total fee | ₹16 lakhs |
| Application fee | ₹1,500 |
| Eligibility | Bachelor's degree in any discipline; final-year students may apply |
| Work experience | Not required (0–2 years preferred) |

**12 specialisation tracks:**

Product & Growth, Business & Strategy Consulting, AI / Data & BI for Managers, B2B Sales Leadership, D2C Growth & Category Management, Startup Leadership & Innovation, GCC Finance, GCC Operations, Digital Marketing, Operations Excellence, Global Business, and Entrepreneurship.

---

### PGP-X — One-Year Post Graduate Program in Management

PGP-X is built for working professionals who want to accelerate into management or make a lateral move into strategy, product, or business leadership. It runs over 3 fast-paced terms plus a 3-month capstone or internship.

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| Duration | 1 year full-time |
| Batch size | 60 students |
| Eligibility | Bachelor's degree + minimum 3 years full-time work experience |
| Target profile | Mid-career professionals; specialists moving into cross-functional management roles |

**10 specialisation tracks** including Product Leadership & Growth Strategy, Business Strategy & Transformation, AI-Driven Decision Making for Managers, GCC Leadership (Finance/Operations), Applied Finance, Commercial & Revenue Leadership, Data & Analytics Leadership, Technology & Digital Transformation, Startup Leadership & Innovation, and Entrepreneurship.

## The AI curriculum advantage

The biggest differentiator at First Bridge is how AI is embedded into the curriculum rather than tacked on as an elective. 60% of courses incorporate AI or data workflows. Students complete 150+ hours of AI training across analytics, automation, and GenAI tools — and none of it requires prior coding knowledge.

The practical outputs matter here. By the time a First Bridge student walks into placement season, they have built dashboards, automated business workflows, analysed datasets, and used GenAI tools across marketing, finance, and operations in real project contexts. That is a different kind of readiness compared to a student who attended a few AI lectures.

The 30 core tools taught span data analysis, business intelligence, automation, and AI-assisted strategy — tools that companies are actively looking for in 2026 hires.

## Industry connect and placements

First Bridge has 80+ corporate partners who are involved in the curriculum at multiple levels — co-designing modules, providing live project briefs, and hiring from the batch. This is the structural reason placements are embedded from Day One rather than being a final-semester scramble.

The curriculum includes 15 live projects per year and 350+ applied learning hours. Students work on real company problem statements throughout the program, and that project performance contributes directly to how they are evaluated during placements.

The 6-month internship in Year 2 (PGP) is structured as a full-time role, not a shadow experience. PGP-X has a 3-month capstone or internship in Term 3, designed around leadership-oriented real business challenges.

The school offers 100% placement assistance. Specific average and highest CTC data for current batches is best confirmed directly with the admissions team through the enquiry form.

## Admission process

The admission process for both PGP and PGP-X is straightforward:

1. **Online application** (application fee: ₹1,500 for PGP)
2. **Personal interview** — for PGP, a standard merit-based interview; for PGP-X, a detailed work profile review followed by a leadership readiness assessment
3. **Merit-based selection** — academic performance and interview performance for PGP; professional experience and leadership potential for PGP-X

Scholarships are available on merit, for diversity and inclusion, and on a need basis.

**Applications are currently open for Batch 2026–28.**

## Frequently asked questions

### What is the fee for First Bridge Business School's PGP program?

The total program fee for the 2-year PGP is ₹16 lakhs. The application fee is ₹1,500. Merit-based scholarships, diversity grants, and need-based financial assistance are available. Contact the admissions team for scholarship eligibility details.

### Do I need work experience to apply for the PGP?

No. The PGP is designed for fresh graduates or candidates with up to 2 years of work experience. Final-year undergraduate students may also apply. For PGP-X, a minimum of 3 years of full-time work experience is required.

### Is coding knowledge required for the AI curriculum?

No. The 150+ hour AI curriculum is designed for managers, not engineers. It covers analytics, GenAI tools, automation, and data-driven decision-making without requiring any programming background.
