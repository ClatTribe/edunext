## Admission Process: Step-by-Step Guide
Here's a clear breakdown of how the First Bridge Business School admission process works for 2026:
* **Step 1: Online Application**
Visit the official EduNext-powered admissions portal and fill in the online application form. Upload the required documents, including mark sheets, entrance exam scores, and a passport-size photograph.

* **Step 2: Shortlisting**
Based on your entrance exam scores, academic performance, and work experience (if applicable), you'll be shortlisted for the next round.

* **Step 3: Written Ability Test (WAT)**
Shortlisted candidates appear for a Written Ability Test assessing communication skills, analytical thinking, and subject knowledge.

* **Step 4: Group Discussion (GD)**
Candidates are evaluated on their ability to articulate ideas, think on their feet, and collaborate — all key traits of future business leaders.

* **Step 5: Personal Interview (PI)**
A final Personal Interview with faculty members and industry professionals determines your overall suitability for the program.

* **Step 6: Offer Letter & Enrollment**
Selected candidates receive an official offer letter and must confirm enrollment by paying the admission fee within the stipulated deadline.
