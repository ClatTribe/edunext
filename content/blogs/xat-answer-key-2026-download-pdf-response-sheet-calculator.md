## How to Download the Official XAT Answer Key?

Exam takers should regularly check the XAT official website and download the answer key immediately after its release. The following steps must be taken to download the XAT Answer Key 2026:

**Step 1:** Visit the official XAT website link: https://xatonline.in/. Check the top right corner of the home page for the XAT Answer Key option.

**Step 2:** Find the link for the Answer Key and click it.

**Step 3:** Fill in the details required on the website and hit enter.

**Step 5:** The information on the answer key should be verified. Also, download and keep a copy of the answer key.

## How to Raise Objections Against XAT Answer Key?

Only candidates can submit contradictory opinions against the XAT Provisional Answer Key. The submission of objections should be made by the candidates only within the deadline given by the authorities. Along with the upload of supporting documents, candidates must ensure that the proofs are valid as well.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1. Is the XAT answer key final?

No. XLRI releases a provisional answer key first. The final answer key is published after resolving objections.

### 2. Does GK affect XAT percentile?

No. GK scores do not affect the percentile but may be considered during the final round.

### 3. When will XAT 2026 results be declared?

The results are usually out 2–3 weeks after the exam.
